📊 Datasets

Dataset 1 – OCT C8

An eight-class Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) dataset containing retinal diseases such as "AMD", "CNV", "CSR", "DME", "DR", "DRUSEN", "MH"and "NORMAL" cases.

Dataset 2 – EyeDisease101

A six-class fundus image dataset including "Cataract", "Glaucoma", Retinal Disease, and other ophthalmic conditions.

Both datasets are split into training, validation, and testing subsets to ensure fair and consistent evaluation.
